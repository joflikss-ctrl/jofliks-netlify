Common Questions

How are photos delivered?

Booked sessions are delivered via a private online gallery link. Digital downloads purchased from the gallery are sent directly to your email immediately after checkout.

What file format do I receive?

All digital downloads are delivered as high-resolution JPEG files, suitable for printing up to 20×30 inches and all digital uses.

Can I use the photos on social media?

Yes — all purchases include a personal and social media use license. Commercial or editorial use requires a separate license; contact for pricing.

Do you travel outside Boston?

Yes! Travel within New England is available. For further distances, a travel fee may apply. Get in touch to discuss your location.

What payment methods are accepted?

All major credit cards, debit cards, and PayPal accepted through secure Gumroad checkout for digital downloads. Invoiced billing available for booked sessions.

Do you offer refunds?

Digital downloads are non-refundable due to the nature of digital goods. For booked sessions, cancellations made 48+ hours in advance receive a full refund.

Custom packages available — reach out and let's build the right solution for you.
